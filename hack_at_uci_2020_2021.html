Hack at UCI
2020 - 2021
Hack at UCI Club Website
hack.ics.uci.edu

Hack at UCI is a student-run organization established to provide students with a platform to learn, grow, and develop technology of the future. The website consists of information about the club, a schedule of events, sponsorship information, and contact information. I designed prototypes for the hackathon's website for both mobile and desktop platforms. Designs were created using Figma.

ZotHacks Website
zothacks.com

ZotHacks is a beginner-friendly hackathon where students with minimal programming experience learn to build their first computer science project. I designed prototypes for the hackathon's website for both mobile and desktop platforms. Designs were created using Figma.

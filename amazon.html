Amazon


During summer 2021, I was a UX design intern for Amazon Alexa. My task was to create a new feature that would improve the value proposition of the already-released Echo Buds 2 and become a why-buy for future iterations of Buds. I designed two new features for buds to improve customer safety on-the-go. These features included gesture and voice design. I presented these designs to stakeholders and iterated on feedback.

In addition to my Echo Buds work, I independently moderated 12 on-site usability studies with an Alexa product prototype to measure customer satisfaction and pain points.

After graduating from UC Irvine, I rejoined as a full-time UX Designer in 2022. To help the Alexa organization generate new long-term goals, my team planned to hold an ideation workshop. For the workshop, I did research to inform customer trends, personas, and unmet needs. I aggregated research from Statista, eMarketer, and internal sources. In addition, I created a survey for qualtrics and a user interview plan for UserTesting.

Overview

As the Design Lead for Hack at UCI, I established the visual direction and brand strategy for one of Orange County’s largest collegiate hackathons. I managed a team of designers to produce all digital and physical assets, including event websites and promotional graphics. By maintaining a high standard of visual quality, our design efforts directly contributed to securing a $2.25K sponsorship from Figma.
